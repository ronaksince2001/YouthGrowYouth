About Us

WHO WE ARE

Founded in September 2021, YouthGrowYouth is a platform that encourages present-day youth to get involved within their community by providing them with personalized opportunities available in today’s market for them to not only build on exceptional college extracurriculars; but also explore and discover career interests. Our programs range from job/volunteering positions, internships, mentorships, exclusive Ivy League programs, and more!

Additionally, being affiliated with the United Nations Youth Council (UNYC) we empower the youth community in achieving gender equality and mass literacy.

OUR STORY

Dear YouthGrowYouth,

Let's rewind to the summer of 2020

Having a dream of wanting to fly abroad and pursue my further education, I was overjoyed at the very thought! I soon began my college research and everything that had to do with application requirements and to my surprise, one of the most important factors was extracurriculars.

I was baffled, “but why do these even matter, aren't my grades good enough”? I questioned myself. Throughout my school era (especially throughout high school) I was so academically oriented that I failed to recognize how incredibly important getting involved in extracurriculars is to build an appealing college application and that grades arent everything.

Fast Foward to September 2021.

I saw my own peer group struggling with finding opportunities to get involved in extracurriculars. There was no platform to discover resources, no access to personalized career-oriented opportunities, and most importantly no community. No longer was my goal to just help my peer group; I instead sought to step boldly into this domain to help several other high school students.

And so I began researching and posting global opportunities on an account called “Youth Grow Youth” in the hopes that this might help someone — anyone.

To my surprise, people began noticing Youth Grow Youth and asked to join the cause. I was ecstatic. We soon blossomed into something greater than I could have ever imagined: we expanded beyond monthly opportunities, creating a global network of chapters, an online community of high school students from across the globe, and three conferences in collaboration with Ivy Leagues, among other impactful initiates.

Today, we stand as a global organization with over 4 chapters across 5 countries, and several accelerators being part of this venture (Harvard University is the most recent accelerator) but more importantly, a safe space all High-schoolers can call Home.

This community has changed my life. It has made me feel more confident and empowered than I’ve ever been. I can only hope that Youth Grow Youth can touch you the same way it did me. I’m honored to share this space with you.


With love,
Simone Sharma
Founder
